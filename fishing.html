One of the many sports I like is fishing. I used to go fishing often but now I go fishing less. This is because I have many studies to complete like Maths, Science and my favourite Digital Technologies. These classes take up time and effort. This website I made for Digital Tech took Me a decent number of hours. What I like most about fishing is just when the fish pulls on the line and I must hook the fish and reel it in. When I reel the line in I must be careful so the line doesn't snap.The line isn't very thick because if it was, the fish would be able to see it and I wouldn't catch anything. My favourite way of fishing is called cast and retrieve. This is when I cast the rod and reel it in slowly. When casting and retrieving, I use a lure because if I used bait, it most likely will come off when I retrieve. I like fishing with my cousin because we sit and talk and fish. It's a great combination!
