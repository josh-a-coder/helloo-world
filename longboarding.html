Mountain-Bike Riding

A great thrill is riding down a hill and not knowing whether a car will drive up the hill or not.
I love this sport, longboarding, the fastest speed I have reached on a longboard is 80km/h.
I was riding my board on a main rode and it felt amasing. One day I was riding my longboard
and I came down a hill at around 50km/h. At the end of the street I did something called a slide,
this where I put my left hand on the road and grab the board with my right hand and turn the board sharply.
This results in the board sliding and slowing down. I wear gloves with plastic pucks so I don't hurt my hands.
I did this thre times and I thought to my-self, "just one more time". So I walked uo the hill one more time and when I came down my foot positioning was incorrect
but I had no choice, I had to slow down or I would fall. So I slid anyway but sadly I fell at 50km/h. Later that day, I arrived at the hospital. I am okay now but that
was a painful experience. One week after I fell, still not fully recovered, I went to the top of the hill and everything went perfectly.
